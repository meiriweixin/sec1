# JC Sciences Syllabus (H2 Level)

**Singapore A-Level H2 Sciences**
- Physics (9749)
- Chemistry (9729)
- Biology (9744)

**Grade Distribution:**
- JC 1: Foundational topics across all three sciences
- JC 2: Advanced topics and applications

**Content Structure:**
Each science will be a **separate subject** in the platform with its own chapters and exercises.

---

## Implementation Plan

### Phase 1: JC 1 Science Structure (Current)
1. Create 3 new subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
2. Add 8 chapters each with lesson content (24 chapters total)
3. Each chapter has 3 comprehensive sections
4. Singapore-specific examples throughout
5. Bilingual support (English + Chinese)

### Phase 2: JC 1 Science Exercises
1. Create 15 exercises per chapter (5 easy, 5 medium, 5 hard)
2. Total: 360 exercises (120 per science)
3. 6-step pedagogical format
4. Smart answer validation
5. MCQ and short answer types

### Phase 3: JC 2 Science Content
1. Follow same pattern as JC 1
2. More advanced topics
3. Application-focused
4. Exam preparation oriented

- Singapore context makes abstract concepts relatable
- Practical applications emphasize real-world relevance
- Each science is a separate subject (not combined like Secondary Science)
- JC students typically take 3-4 H2 subjects including Mathematics
- Content aligns with Singapore-Cambridge A-Level syllabus
