**Question 8** [8 marks]

Mr. Tan is planning a family trip from Singapore to Kuala Lumpur. He has two options:

**Option A: Drive**
- Distance: 350 km
- Average speed: 80 km/h
- Petrol consumption: 12 km per litre
- Petrol price: $\$2.50$ per litre
- Toll fees: $\$45$

**Option B: Take the bus**
- Adult ticket: $\$45$ per person
- Child ticket (under 12): $\$25$ per person
- Journey time: 5 hours

Mr. Tan's family consists of 2 adults and 2 children.

(a) Calculate the total driving time for Option A. [1]

(b) Calculate the amount of petrol needed for Option A. [1]

(c) Calculate the total cost of Option A (petrol + tolls). [2]

(d) Calculate the total cost of Option B for the family. [1]

(e) Which option is cheaper and by how much? [1]

(f) If Mr. Tan drives at 100 km/h instead, how much time would he save compared to the bus? [2]

```

    |            |
    |            |
    D____________C
```

The rectangular lawn $ABCD$ has length 20 m and width 12 m.
The semi-circular flower bed has a diameter equal to the width of the lawn.

(a) Calculate the area of the rectangular lawn. [1]

(b) Calculate the area of the semi-circular flower bed. [2]

(c) Find the total area of the garden. [1]

(d) Mr. Lim wants to fence the entire perimeter of the garden (the three straight sides of the rectangle plus the curved part of the semi-circle). Calculate the total length of fencing needed. [2]

**Question 11** [7 marks]

A water tank in the shape of a rectangular prism has internal dimensions 80 cm by 60 cm by 50 cm.

(a) Calculate the capacity of the tank in litres. [2]

(b) Water flows into the empty tank at a rate of 12 litres per minute. How long will it take to fill the tank completely? [2]

(d) Once full, the tank loses 2% of its water through evaporation each day. How many litres of water remain after 3 days? Give your answer to 1 decimal place. [1]
