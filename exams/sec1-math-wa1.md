**Question 12** [6 marks]

A hawker uncle at a Singapore hawker centre sells chicken rice for $\$x$ per plate and drinks for $\$y$ each.

(a) Write an algebraic expression for the total cost of 3 plates of chicken rice and 5 drinks. [1]

(b) If the hawker sells 50 plates of chicken rice and 80 drinks in a day, write an expression for his daily revenue. [1]

(c) On Monday, he sold $p$ plates of chicken rice and $(p + 20)$ drinks. On Tuesday, he sold $(p + 15)$ plates of chicken rice and $(p - 5)$ drinks. Write a simplified expression for the total number of items sold over both days. [2]

(d) Given that the total items sold was 200, find the value of $p$. [2]

---

**Question 13** [8 marks]

Three MRT trains A, B and C leave Jurong East station at the same time.
- Train A returns to Jurong East every 8 minutes.
- Train B returns every 12 minutes.
- Train C returns every 18 minutes.

(a) Find the LCM of 8, 12 and 18. [2]

(b) After how many minutes will all three trains be at Jurong East station together again? [1]

(c) If the first train leaves at 6:00 a.m., at what time will all three trains next be at the station together? [1]

(d) How many times will all three trains be at the station together between 6:00 a.m. and 12:00 noon (inclusive of 6:00 a.m. but not 12:00 noon)? [2]

(e) Train D also starts from Jurong East at 6:00 a.m. All four trains are at the station together at 9:00 a.m. for the first time. How often does Train D return to Jurong East? Give all possible answers. [2]
