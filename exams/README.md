# Secondary 1 Mathematics Examination Papers

## Overview

This folder contains comprehensive examination papers for Secondary 1 Mathematics aligned with the Singapore MOE G2/G3 syllabus. The papers are designed for 4 major assessment periods throughout the academic year.

| Assessment | Duration | Total Marks | Calculator | Topics Covered |
|------------|----------|-------------|------------|----------------|
| **WA1** | 1 hour | 50 marks | Not Allowed | Chapters 1-4 |
| **WA2** | 1 hour | 50 marks | Allowed | Chapters 4-8 |
| **WA3** | 1 hour | 50 marks | Allowed | Chapters 9-12 |
| **EOY** | 2.5 hours | 100 marks | Paper 1: No, Paper 2: Yes | All Chapters |

## Marking Scheme Guidelines

### General Principles

1. **Method Marks (M)**: Awarded for correct approach even if final answer is wrong
2. **Accuracy Marks (A)**: Awarded for correct final answers
3. **Bonus Marks (B)**: Occasionally given for exceptional solutions

### Specific Guidelines

- **Show Working**: Questions requiring working get 0 marks for answer-only responses
- **Units**: Penalize missing or wrong units in mensuration questions
- **Significant Figures**: Unless specified, accept 3 s.f. or exact answers
- **Equivalent Forms**: Accept equivalent algebraic expressions
- **Graph Tolerance**: Allow Â±1 small square for graph questions

## Notes for Teachers

2. **Timing**: Allow extra time for students with learning needs
3. **Calculator Policy**: Ensure students know which paper allows calculators
4. **Formula Sheet**: No formula sheet provided (students must memorize)
5. **Answer Booklets**: Use lined paper for working, graph paper for graphs
