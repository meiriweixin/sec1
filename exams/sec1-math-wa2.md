**Question 11** [6 marks]

Mr. Tan bought a HDB flat for $\$450,000$. After 5 years, the value of the flat appreciated by 20%.

(a) Find the value of the flat after 5 years. [1]

(b) Mr. Tan then sold the flat and made a profit of 15% on the appreciated value. Find the selling price. [2]

(c) Calculate Mr. Tan's overall percentage profit based on his original purchase price. [2]

(d) If he had sold it at a 10% loss instead, what would the selling price have been? [1]

---

**Question 12** [7 marks]

A recipe for making 12 curry puffs requires:
- 300 g of flour
- 200 g of potatoes
- 150 g of chicken
- 4 eggs

(a) Find the ratio of flour to potatoes to chicken in its simplest form. [1]

(b) Mrs. Lim wants to make 30 curry puffs for a party. Calculate the amount of each ingredient she needs. [2]

(c) Flour costs $\$2.50$ per kg, potatoes cost $\$1.80$ per kg, chicken costs $\$8.00$ per kg, and eggs cost $\$0.30$ each. Find the cost of ingredients to make 30 curry puffs. [2]

(d) If Mrs. Lim sells each curry puff for $\$1.50$, calculate her percentage profit. [2]

---

**Question 13** [7 marks]

A water tank can be filled by Pipe A alone in 6 hours and by Pipe B alone in 4 hours.

(a) What fraction of the tank can Pipe A fill in 1 hour? [1]

(b) What fraction of the tank can Pipe B fill in 1 hour? [1]

(c) If both pipes are turned on together, what fraction of the tank is filled in 1 hour? [1]

(d) How long will it take to fill the tank if both pipes are used together? [2]

(e) Pipe C can drain the full tank in 8 hours. If all three pipes are turned on with the tank initially empty, how long will it take to fill the tank? [2]
