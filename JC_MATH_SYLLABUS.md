# Singapore JC Mathematics Syllabus (H2 Level)

## JC 1 Topics (First Year)

### 1. Functions and Graphs
- Graphical transformations
- Rational functions and their graphs
- Modulus functions
- Composite and inverse functions

### 2. Equations and Inequalities
- Conditions for quadratic equations to have real roots
- Graphical solution of inequalities
- Modulus inequalities

### 3. Sequences and Series
- Arithmetic and geometric progressions
- Sum to n terms and sum to infinity
- Applications and problem solving

### 4. Vectors in 2D and 3D
- Vector operations (addition, scalar multiplication)
- Position vectors and displacement
- Ratio theorem
- Scalar product

### 5. Differentiation Techniques
- Product rule, quotient rule, chain rule
- Derivatives of trigonometric, exponential, logarithmic functions
- Parametric differentiation
- Implicit differentiation

### 6. Applications of Differentiation
- Tangents and normals
- Connected rates of change
- Maxima and minima problems
- Curve sketching

## JC 2 Topics (Second Year)

### 7. Integration Techniques
- Standard integrals
- Integration by substitution
- Integration by parts
- Partial fractions

### 8. Applications of Integration
- Area under curve
- Volume of revolution
- Differential equations

### 9. Maclaurin Series
- Series expansions
- Approximations
- Applications

### 10. Probability
- Permutations and combinations
- Probability of events
- Conditional probability
- Binomial distribution

### 11. Sampling and Hypothesis Testing
- Normal distribution
- Central limit theorem
- Confidence intervals
- Hypothesis tests

### 12. Complex Numbers
- Operations with complex numbers
- Argand diagram
- Modulus-argument form
- De Moivre's theorem
