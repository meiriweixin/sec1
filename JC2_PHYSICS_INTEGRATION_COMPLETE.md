## üî¨ Sample Content Quality

### Example: Chapter 1 (Thermodynamics) - Exercise Quality

**Exercise ID:** `thermo-jc2-ex4`
**Type:** Short answer
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Question:**
"Gas does 300J of work while absorbing 800J of heat. Change in internal energy ŒîU = Q - W?"

**6-Step Pedagogical Explanation:**

1. **Problem:** Calculate change in internal energy from heat and work
2. **Key Concept:** First law of thermodynamics: ŒîU = Q - W
3. **Steps:**
   - Q = +800 J (heat absorbed, positive)
   - W = +300 J (work done by gas, positive)
   - ŒîU = 800 - 300 = 500 J
4. **Answer:** 500 J
5. **Common Mistakes:** Sign convention errors. Heat absorbed is positive, work done by gas is positive in this convention.
6. **Tip:** NEWater plants use heat exchangers operating on these thermodynamic principles!

**Singapore Context:** References NEWater plant operations, making abstract physics concepts tangible through local infrastructure.
